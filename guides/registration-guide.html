Course Registration Guide 选课指南

Disclaimer (as of 202508): This is not an official note, only a kind reminder.

Before You Register:

  • 检查你的选课时间 (Check your registration time)
  • 清除所有选课限制 (Clear any registration bars)
  • 查看下方详细检查清单 (Detailed checklist below)
  • 规划你的课程 (Plan your courses)

详细Registration Bar检查清单 (Detailed Registration Bar Checklist)

首先检查 RIS (Registration Information Sheet): 确认所有选课的bar都已消除
(First check RIS (Registration Information Sheet): Confirm all course selection bars have been cleared)

Check Registration Information Sheet


Financial Bar

解决方法: 前往 What I Owe 页面付款即可消除
(Solution: Go to the What I Owe page to make payment and it will be cleared)

Medical Bar

国际学生需要特别注意疫苗相关的bar
(International students need to pay special attention to vaccine-related bars)

TB Test特别提醒: TB test有效期较短，大部分同学都需要重做。国内可能需要预约，国外可以去clinic做。等结果时间较长，需要提早准备。
(TB Test Special Reminder: TB test has a short validity period, most students need to retake it. In China, you may need to make an appointment, abroad you can go to a clinic. Results take a long time, so prepare early.)

International Bar

美高学生需要注意I-20状态。如果是直接从美高transfer的I-20，需要跟International Office更新。
(American high school students need to pay attention to I-20 status. If it's an I-20 directly transferred from American high school, you need to update it with the International Office.)

Non-financial Bar

需要找给你设bar的office联系即可消除
(You need to contact the office that set the bar to have it cleared)

Advising Bar

给advisor发邮件或者约meeting即可消除
(Send an email to your advisor or schedule a meeting to have it cleared)
